The *kirigami* (the art of adding cuts to paper) shell used in the grasping tool showcased a smooth closing behavior that allowed it to pick up delicate objects. I added various valley and mountain folds to the convex side of kirigami gripper to enable a snapping behavior not previous observed. This new shell is called the *Ori-Kirigami* shell (adding *origami*, the art of adding folds to paper, to the aforementioned *kirigami*).
