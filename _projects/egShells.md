## Grasping Delicate Objects with Kirigami Grippers
**Tools/Skills:** *3D printing (FDM), CAD (OnShape, SolidWorks), Dynamic Mechancial Testing (Instron machine), Laser Cutter, Silicone Molding*

Grasping a fragile object without damaging it is a common challenge both in every day life and industrial use. Many solutions focus on making sophisticated appendages that mimic those in nature: finger-like structures guided by sensors and actuators. Here we have created a soft, compliant gripper inspired by *kirigami*, the Japanese art of applying cuts to paper, that is able to conform to the target object by deforming itself. 

Improvements made:
- 6X increase in load capacity by reinforcing areas of greatest deformation
- 8X increase in fatigue life by reinforcing areas of high stress concentration
- An intuitive handheld tool for easy operation
